Privacy Policy for FakeUA Browse No Data Collection

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

⚠️ Important Notice: FakeUA Browse does NOT collect any personal data. This app operates entirely on your device and does not send any information to us or any third party.

Thank you for using FakeUA Browse (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to your privacy. As a User Agent (UA) spoofing tool designed for web developers and testers, we prioritize user privacy and data protection.

1. No Data Collection Policy

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data from your device.

  • No Personal Data: We do not require or collect any personal information such as name, email, location, contacts, or any identifiable data.
  • No Browsing Data: As a UA spoofing browser, the App allows you to change your User Agent string for development testing. All browsing activity, history, cookies, and cache remain strictly on your device. We cannot access this data.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: The App contains no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking services (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.). No usage data, crash reports, or device information is collected.
  • No Network Proxying: Unlike proxy/VPN apps, FakeUA Browse only modifies the HTTP User-Agent header. It does not route or proxy your internet traffic through external servers.

2. How FakeUA Browse Works

This app is a specialized browser tool for web development:

  • Local UA Spoofing: User Agent strings are modified locally on your device before requests are sent to websites.
  • No Server Involvement: All UA modifications happen client-side. No requests go through our servers.
  • Preset & Custom UAs: You can select from preset UA strings or create custom ones. These settings are stored locally in your device's UserDefaults.
  • WebKit-Based: Uses Apple's native WebKit engine (WKWebView) for secure browsing within iOS sandbox.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data stays on your device:

  • Local Storage Only: Any saved UA profiles, bookmarks, or browsing data are stored locally using iOS sandbox storage.
  • No Cloud Sync: The App does not sync data to iCloud or any cloud service.
  • Automatic Clearing: Browsing session data (cache, cookies) can be cleared manually via app settings or automatically upon app termination (based on iOS settings).
  • iOS Security: The app inherits iOS security features including sandboxing, data protection, and secure network communication.

4. Third-Party Websites

When visiting websites through the App:

  • External Cookies & Tracking: Websites you visit may set their own cookies and tracking technologies. We have no control over these.
  • Website Privacy Policies: External sites operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.
  • HTTPS Enforcement: The App encourages secure connections where available but cannot guarantee website security practices.
